Meet Our Pastor

  • Apostle Oladele Abolarin founded the Divine Glorious Bible Ministry (DGBMI) in October 2013. Empowered and inspired by God, he continues to lead this wonderful ministry and dedicate his life to bringing souls to Christ every day

    Our Core Objectives:

    Ignite a passion in every soul to follow Christ.

    Preach the gospel of the Kingdom of God across the globe until His return.

    Connect the hearts of unbelievers to the living, powerful presence of God.

Our Vision
